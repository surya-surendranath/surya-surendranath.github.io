MINDFULNESS

1.What did you learn from your mindfulness conversation?How did the other person find it?

From the mindfulness conversation with my friend I felt like, We can’t exchange something with someone when they, or we, are not present. We can talk to them, we can talk at them, but we can’t have a conversation. After listening to my mindfulness talk my friend said that "something worth hearing helps make great conversation. We love it when we’re affected by what we hear. So If you’re not being heard, don’t blame the listener. Go and come up with something that engages them more".

2.Which of the three suggested technique will you use to sustain your practice?

I may use mindful breathing along with meditation.

3.Any other musings?

Mindful listening is very effective to stay happy and active because We do not believe in ourselves until someone reveals that something deep inside us is valuable, worth listening to, worthy of our trust, sacred to our touch. Once we believe in ourselves we can risk curiosity, wonder, spontaneous delight or any experience that reveals the human spirit.
