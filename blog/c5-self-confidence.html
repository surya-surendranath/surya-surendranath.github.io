MINDFULNESS

1.What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

Mindfulness is often seen as a synonym for awareness, I believe mindfulness is more of a subset.Practicing mindfulness is practicing a type of awareness that is thoroughly accepting, highly self inspecting, and intensely focused on qualification and quantification of the subject of that awareness. It's almost like awareness squared. Awareness is a little more vague of a term. It can be used in the sense of non-dual awareness, where the distinction between observer and observed fades away, or choiceless awareness, the state of mind where you drop all intention and aren't drawn towards or away from any object.
