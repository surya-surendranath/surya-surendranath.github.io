SELF CONTROL

1.What learning did you take from this chapter?

I felt like self-control is a crucial component of emotional intelligence. Emotions provide us with energy and drive, and can communicate important information. Self-control allows to stop ourself from doing things you want to do but that might not be in your best interest. For example, without self-control, you might burp and curse nonstop.

2.How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

Mindfulness is one of the most effective ways to strengthen our mental health and gain control over our emotions. Mindfulness involves creating an awareness of your body, thoughts and feelings. The more awareness of your thoughts and feelings you develop, the more control you gain over them. After practising Mindfulness through Meditation for a while, I have noticed that I become Mindful of my body, thoughts and feelings at times throughout the day. The ultimate goal within Mindfulness is to develop our Mindfulness to the point where we have a greater self-awareness in all of your daily interactions and activities.
